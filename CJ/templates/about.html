India Gate. See for yourself.

India Gate, official name Delhi Memorial, originally called All-India War Memorial, monumental sandstone arch in New Delhi, dedicated to the troops of British India who died in wars fought between 1914 and 1919.

India Gate was designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens and was built in 1931 and initially named as 'All India War Memorial'. Inspired by the Arc de Triomphe in Paris and constructed in red sandstone and granite, India Gate stands 42 m tall. .
